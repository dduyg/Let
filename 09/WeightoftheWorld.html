Weight Of The World

Scene: [INT. SMALL APARTMENT - NIGHT]

Jaehyun stands by the window, staring at the city lights, his shoulders heavy.

JAEHYUN: (softly)
I thought I could carry it all.

Eunji enters, placing a hand on his shoulder.

EUNJI: You don’t have to. I’m here.

Jaehyun looks up, his eyes full of gratitude but still burdened.

JAEHYUN: I don't know how to let go.

[FADE OUT.]
