Wings of Tomorrow

Eun-joo (late 20s, introverted, determined) is a successful, yet lonely, architect living in Seoul. She has a perfect career but is struggling to connect with her emotions after a traumatic event in her past. Despite her accomplishments, her heart is closed off, and she constantly feels like something is missing.

One day, while working late in her office, Eun-joo encounters Ji-hoon (early 30s, warm, charming, but secretive). Ji-hoon is a mysterious man who appears out of nowhere, claiming to be a fallen angel sent from the future. He tells Eun-joo that she is destined to help him repair his broken wings in order to save the world from an impending disaster that will occur in 30 days.

At first, Eun-joo thinks Ji-hoon is delusional, but as strange and supernatural events start to unfold, she begins to believe him. Her once-sturdy world is upended as she becomes involved in a love story that transcends time and space. Alongside Ji-hoon, she embarks on a journey of discovery, not only about the future but also about her own hidden strength and emotions.

As they grow closer, Eun-joo finds herself torn between two worlds: her duty to help Ji-hoon repair his wings and her blossoming feelings for him. Meanwhile, Ji-hoon must confront his own painful past, and the reason his wings are broken is much more personal than he initially lets on. The clock is ticking, and both are forced to confront the idea of sacrifice, love, and the future they could create together.

Episode 1-4: The Unexpected Encounter

EP1: Eun-joo, a successful yet emotionally distant architect, is working late at her office when she meets Ji-hoon, a mysterious man who claims to be a fallen angel. He tells her that she is the key to fixing his broken wings and preventing a disaster from happening in the future. Eun-joo dismisses him as delusional but is intrigued by his knowledge of her life.

EP2: As strange occurrences start happening around Eun-joo, she finds herself inadvertently pulled into Ji-hoon's world. He reveals that he has been sent from the future to fix his wings, which are tied to the fate of the world. Though reluctant, Eun-joo agrees to help him after witnessing an inexplicable supernatural event. Meanwhile, she begins to feel a connection to him despite her reservations.

EP3: Ji-hoon begins showing up unexpectedly in Eun-joo’s life, claiming that they are connected through fate. He starts helping her with seemingly small but meaningful tasks that bring emotional growth for Eun-joo. As they grow closer, Eun-joo’s best friend, Min-seo, becomes suspicious of Ji-hoon and warns Eun-joo to be careful.

EP4: Eun-joo discovers that her painful past—an unresolved relationship with her first love—has been blocking her ability to open up to others. Ji-hoon becomes a source of comfort and guidance. However, as their bond deepens, strange visions of the future start appearing to both of them, suggesting that their relationship may have a greater significance than they realize.

Episode 5-8: Love and Secrets

EP5: Ji-hoon reveals more about his mission: his wings were broken during an incident involving a love he had in the past, which has now set a disastrous chain of events in motion. He needs Eun-joo’s help to fix the wings, but it requires a personal sacrifice. Meanwhile, Eun-joo’s feelings for him begin to intensify, though she tries to fight them due to her fear of getting hurt again.

EP6: As Eun-joo and Ji-hoon’s relationship deepens, Eun-joo starts experiencing strange dreams where she witnesses scenes from Ji-hoon’s past life. She is both drawn to him and terrified by the implications of their connection. Min-seo starts to investigate Ji-hoon’s background, growing more concerned about the nature of his true mission.

EP7: Ji-hoon confronts his past lover, Yoo-na, who reveals painful truths about his fall from grace. It turns out that Ji-hoon’s love for Yoo-na was what led to the breaking of his wings. Eun-joo learns that her role in this mission is tied to the unresolved tragedy between Ji-hoon and Yoo-na. Meanwhile, Ji-hoon tries to distance himself from Eun-joo, fearing he’ll hurt her in the same way.

EP8: Eun-joo’s emotional walls begin to crumble as she opens up about her traumatic past, including the heartbreak she suffered when her first love disappeared without a trace. As she helps Ji-hoon confront his own heartache, they share a moment of deep connection, strengthening their bond. However, Ji-hoon is torn between protecting Eun-joo from the inevitable heartbreak and staying true to his mission.

Episode 9-12: The Countdown Begins

EP9: The 30-day countdown to the disaster begins. Eun-joo and Ji-hoon must work together to uncover the key to fixing Ji-hoon’s wings. The clock is ticking, and the stakes grow higher. Eun-joo faces an emotional battle, torn between her growing love for Ji-hoon and the knowledge that their time together is limited.

EP10: Ji-hoon confesses his feelings to Eun-joo, but he warns her that falling in love with him means sacrificing something precious. Eun-joo struggles with the truth of his words, but she can’t deny her feelings. Meanwhile, Min-seo becomes increasingly worried and uncovers a hidden connection between Eun-joo’s family and Ji-hoon’s mission.

EP11: Eun-joo and Ji-hoon discover that the disaster Ji-hoon must prevent is tied to a decision Eun-joo will make in the near future. The events that lead to the disaster are set into motion, and Eun-joo is forced to confront her own role in the future. She faces the choice of whether to follow her heart or make a selfless decision to protect Ji-hoon and the world.

EP12: In a pivotal moment, Ji-hoon must make a painful sacrifice to fix his wings, and Eun-joo must decide whether she will continue to help him or let go for good. Their connection is tested, and a shocking revelation about Ji-hoon’s true identity emerges, leading to a dramatic cliffhanger that leaves both their fates in the balance.
