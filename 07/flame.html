Oh Ataturk, father of the land,
With wisdom and courage, you took a stand.
For freedom and progress, you paved the way,
Your legacy shines bright, even to this day.

Through battles and hardship, he never lost his stride,
A leader and a warrior, with honor as his guide.
He taught us to be fearless, to dream and to aspire,
To build a better future, to always reach higher.

Through the storm and the struggle, he forged a path of light,
For a nation born from shadows, emerging in the night. For the father of our nation, we lift our voices high,
Mustafa Kemal Ataturk, forever in our sky.

In the hearts of the people, his spirit still remains,
Guiding us through trials, through sunshine and the rains.
Mustafa Kemal Ataturk, your vision led the way,
We honor you in memory, every single day.

From the past to the future, your light will always guide,
In the soul of Turkey, your dreams will never die.
Mustafa Kemal Ataturk, your name forever strong,
In every heart that beats, you live on in our song.
