It creates tasks, like predicting missing parts of data or detecting transformations, to learn representations without explicit human annotation. SSL bridges the gap between supervised and unsupervised learning, enabling models to pretrain on vast unlabeled datasets and generalize better to downstream tasks. This approach has shown success in areas like natural language processing and computer vision, significantly reducing reliance on labeled data.
